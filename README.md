This repository contains the Python scripts to download the raw data at the basis of the analysis presented in:

Di Donato, F., Mannocci, A., Pecoraro, F., & Provost, L. (2026). Mind the Gap: From Policy to Practice at CNR. An Analysis of Career Advancement Criteria in Light of the Agreement on Reforming Research Assessment. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18642800


The raw data has been used during out stury to produce the supplementary material of the aforementioned research paper, which can be found here:

Di Donato, F., Mannocci, A., Pecoraro, F., & Provost, L. (2026). Supplemental material to the article 'Mind the Gap: From Policy to Practice at CNR. An Analysis of Career Advancement Criteria in Light of the Agreement on Reforming Research Assessment' (1.0) [Data set]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18657087
